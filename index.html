How we met // Notre rencontre

Our beautiful story began in 2016 in Paris.
The probability of our paths crossing was small. And yet, we don’t consider today to live our lives without each other.
That day, we went to a costume party. High in color in her Brazilian outfit for one, gray Bugs Bunny suit for the other, destiny has brought us together at the same table. We laughed a lot, talked and even danced. While our lives seemed to be poles apart, it seemed obvious that we had the same tastes and comparable sensitivity.
A man from the countryside, a woman from the city, we knew how to connect our lives, our desires, our projects.
Chance or Destiny? We do not really know, we live our feelings every day with the same intensity.
