How we met // Notre rencontre

Our beautiful story began in 2016 in Paris.
The probability of our paths crossing was small. And yet, we don’t consider today to live our lives without each other.
That day, we went to a costume party. High in color in her Brazilian outfit for one, gray Bugs Bunny suit for the other, destiny has brought us together at the same table. We laughed a lot, talked and even danced. While our lives seemed to be poles apart, it seemed obvious that we had the same tastes and comparable sensitivity.
A man from the countryside, a woman from the city, we knew how to connect our lives, our desires, our projects.
Chance or Destiny? We do not really know, we live our feelings every day with the same intensity.

Traditional Indian ceremony - Brunch // Cérémonie traditionnelle indienne - Brunch
11h15-16h00

It is the main ceremony in any Indian wedding. The moment when the groom and bride meet around a sacred fire where they are declared husband and wife. At the end of this ceremony, the groom encircles his wife's neck with a necklace, a sign of eternal love. Then, a brunch buffet based on the two cultures will bring your taste buds back to life. C’est la cérémonie principale dans tout mariage indien. Le moment où le marié et la mariée se retrouve autour d’un feu sacré où ils sont déclarés mari et femme. A l’issu de cette cérémonie, le marié encercle le cou de sa femme d’un collier, signe d’un amour éternel. Ensuite, un brunch buffet sur la base des deux cultures ravivera vos papilles.
